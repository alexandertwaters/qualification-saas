Fruthermore. I want the equipment selection (cohort and type) to reflect more natural and real world language. Use case and capabilities selections should also reflect more natural and real world language.
- **Protocol draft generation** — Needs improvement.
- **In-site draft view** — Human-readable HTML preview in a view panel
- **Word (.docx) download** — Downloadable protocol draft. i want download PDF added.
- **Equipment catalog API** — `/api/equipment-catalog` for UI
- **Draft API** — `POST /api/draft` (JSON or Word)
- **Security** - Need to understand current state of security/hardening measures. Do not want bot signups, do not want false invoice generation and fake orders. Do not want draft preview or draft protocol generation without payment. only real users, real buyers, users can reset own password, i recieve email on orders completed and user recieves invoice. User has an issue with draft generation or allowance or payment, that is real, they can use an automated pathway to submit a help request, etc. all needs creation and implementation.
- **Advisory positioning** — Notice that users bear responsibility. needs hardening.
